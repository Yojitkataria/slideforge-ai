- Leadership wants measurable AI impact within 12 months.
- Current process inefficiencies create a 14% cost drag.

2. Automate repetitive operational tasks.
3. Launch AI copilots for internal teams.
4. Establish AI governance and risk controls.

## Expected Outcomes
- Operating cost reduction: 12%
- Forecast accuracy improvement: 18%
- Support resolution time reduction: 35%
- Revenue uplift from personalization: 6%
