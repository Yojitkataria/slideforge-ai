# Operations Improvement Plan

## Problem Statement
Operational delays are affecting customer experience, delivery timelines, and margin performance.

## Baseline Metrics
- Average lead time: 14 days
- On-time delivery: 82%
- Defect rate: 5.6%
- Escalations per month: 126

## Root Cause Breakdown
| Issue | Impact | Frequency | Owner |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Manual handoffs | High | 42 | Operations |
| Inconsistent SOP usage | Medium | 31 | Quality |
| Incomplete order data | High | 28 | Sales Ops |
| Capacity bottlenecks | Medium | 19 | Planning |

## 90-Day Action Plan
1. Standardize intake and data validation checks.
2. Automate handoffs and SLA alerts.
3. Introduce weekly quality review cadence.
4. Deploy capacity balancing dashboard.

## Target Metrics by Day 90
- Lead time: 10 days
- On-time delivery: 92%
- Defect rate: 3.5%
- Escalations per month: below 70
