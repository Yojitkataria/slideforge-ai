# Q1 Performance Review

Q1 closed with $12.4M revenue and 9.8% quarter-over-quarter growth.
Pipeline conversion improved from 21% to 27%.

## Key Insights
- East shows highest customer satisfaction (NPS 64).
- South has the highest churn and needs retention playbooks.
- North contributes the largest revenue share.

## Next Quarter Focus
1. Reduce churn below 3%.
2. Increase conversion to 30%.
3. Expand enterprise upsell motions.
